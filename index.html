About Voiz

Our Mission

Voiz is designed to create a transparent communication channel between students and campus administration. We believe that every student's voice matters, and by providing a safe, anonymous platform, we empower students to speak up about issues that matter to them.

How It Works

  1. Students post anonymous feedback about campus issues
  2. Other students can upvote issues they find important
  3. Posts are automatically routed to the appropriate department
  4. Administrators can track and update the status of each issue
  5. Students can see when their issues are resolved

Why It Matters

Traditional feedback systems often fail because students fear retaliation or don't believe their concerns will be addressed. Whisper Voiz removes these barriers by providing anonymity and transparency, ensuring that even the smallest whispers can lead to meaningful change on campus.
